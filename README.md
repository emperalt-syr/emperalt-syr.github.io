## About the Project

FirstPath is a mobile app designed for first-generation immigrant students who struggle to find scholarships, financial aid, and campus resources. This repository documents the full HCI design process across three phases — needfinding, design research, and high-fidelity prototyping.

## Adding Prototype Screenshots

In `prototyping.html`, replace the placeholder boxes with your actual Figma screenshots by updating the `screen-mock` divs with `<img>` tags:

```html
<img src="images/screen-task1-onboarding.png" alt="Onboarding screen" style="width:100%; height:200px; object-fit:cover;">
```

Place your image files in an `images/` folder in the repository root.

---

## Three Primary Tasks

1. **Finding and saving a resource** — Personalized feed of scholarships filtered by the student's background and major
2. **Using the eligibility checker** — Four-question form that tells students whether they qualify before they invest time applying
3. **Setting a deadline alert** — Proactive push notification with a peer tip from a student who applied and won
