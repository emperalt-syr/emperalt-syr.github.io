CSE 400 — HCI Design Portfolio

Access to opportunity shouldn't depend on who you know.

FirstPath is a mobile app designed to help first-generation immigrant students find scholarships, check eligibility, and never miss a deadline again.

👤 Emmanuel Peralta Castro 🎓 Syracuse University 📱 Mobile App Design
The Problem

The resources exist. The access doesn't.

First-generation immigrant students make up a massive and growing share of higher education, yet they consistently miss out on scholarships, financial aid, and campus support. The problem isn't a lack of resources — it's that those resources are invisible to the students who need them most, and access is largely determined by luck and who you happen to know.

🔍

Invisible resources

Scholarships, tutoring, and financial aid exist but are scattered across offices and portals not designed for first-gen students to navigate alone.

⏰

Missed deadlines

Students find out about opportunities after the window has already closed — not because they forgot, but because they never knew the deadline existed.

🤝

No trusted guide

Students with college-educated parents get informal coaching their peers never receive. FirstPath brings that guidance to everyone.

The Solution

Three things FirstPath does well

🎯

Personalized discovery

Enter your background once and get a short, filtered list of scholarships and resources that actually apply to you — in plain language, not jargon.

✅

Eligibility checker

Answer four quick questions before investing time in an application. Know in under a minute whether something is worth pursuing.

🔔

Proactive alerts

Set a reminder and FirstPath notifies you before the deadline — with a peer tip from a student who already applied successfully.

Design Process

How we got here

📋

Phase 1 — Needfinding

User research using Reddit communities, NCES data, Pew Research reports, and AI-assisted needfinding to surface real pain points and user needs.

✏️

Phase 2 — Design Research

Task analysis, persona development, journey mapping, and three storyboards covering the core tasks of the app.

📱

Phase 3 — Prototype & Evaluation

High-fidelity Figma prototype across three tasks, followed by a heuristic evaluation and final revisions.
